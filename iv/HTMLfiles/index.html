Shuchi Soumya
Deloitte

The selection process started with an online test. The test was divided into three categories, quants, English and reasoning and there was a time limit for each section. Since I'd been preparing for CAT, the questions were fairly easy for me, although I would advise you to study a little for this if you haven't prepared for anything similar. The questions are basic so you just need to brush up your concepts that you studied in school.
After clearing the test, I moved on to the interview. The interview was short and to the point and the questions were based on your CV. There was a case study given to every candidate. My case study was to suggest an idea for an online clothing store like myntra. Some HR questions are also there. The key to every interview is to be confident in what you say and sell yourself to the recruiter to the best of your ability.
We got the results of the interview the same day and luckily I was selected.
That was my experience with Deloitte. I hope it helps you in some way. All the best!

Hey, I am from CAPP (2018-21). SAP is the company I going to join on 2nd August 2021. There were many reason of choosing SAP over others especially over Deloitte; even many of my mates and juniors asked why I choose 1.98LPA over 3.82LPA, its not just only that SAP is proving M.tech through WILP program of BITS Pilani, but there are many more reasons for that especially the selection process.

In managerial round, she asked me about SAP, why do I want to join SAP and few other questions.

Infosys

Hey everyone I am Rahul and I would like to share my experience of being placed in INFOSYS with you all. So, the story like this, we were in the lockdown period as we all know that it was 2020. As I was in the IT field so I knew that we will be getting the placement offers from different companies. I knew about some of the companies like Wipro, Infosys, Deloitte, etc. that they will be coming to our college for placements. So, I decided to get placed in one of them. So, I stared my preparations for these companies. I did not take classes for these but I opted the other option that was YouTube. I searched for different subjects for preparations on YouTube and got many suggestions and started with Quantitative aptitude and followed single channel for one type like aptitude and for reasoning the other channels. They provided mock test and pdfs for preparations. So, I was preparing from their stuffs. As I got time I used to study for placements. I only studied for 3 hours and not more than that.
So, after few weeks we got the message that Infosys has came for placement in our college, I filled the form and uploaded my resume with it and provided my other details like email id and phone number. After that in a month I got the mail by the Infosys stating the date for examination which was going to be conducted online. They sent the id and password for the same and a link for the test. I was a bit nervous as I never had an online examination experience before. Still I appeared for the test. The test consisted three sections the aptitude, reasoning and the English section. One can attempt only one section at a time. So, I started with the aptitude section and completed it. After that they gave 2 min break before I could start the next section. I completed all the three sections and closed the tab. After few mins I got the mail saying the I have successfully completed and submitted the test.
After one or two months I got the mail saying that I have qualified the test and the next round will be the technical interview round. Infosys has only two rounds that is the test and the technical interview round. Infosys sends you mail 48 hours before conducting the interview so you can be prepared. Now it was the final round and I was a bit scared too still I have to do it. So the interview started and the interviewer asked me to introduce myself and I did it. Then he asked me in which language you are more comfortable and I said in C++. Then he asked me questions from c++ like explain inheritance, data binding, and so on. So, my all over experience really good. After all these questions they asked me about reallocation and I said I am ready for it. And this is how my interview ended.
After few weeks I got the mail saying that I have cleared my interview round too. This is how I got placed in Infosys. I hope this helps you. If you have any queries regarding this please do ask. Thank you and all the best to you all!

Navmeet Tripathy
Wipro

A student of BSc (Info Tech). Recently got placed in Wipro Limited, one of the most recognized company in the IT space. I know a lot of students carry their dream of getting placed and work in top MNCs. Wipro is a popular choice among freshers who desire to build their career in IT firms. So, keeping it all short, I’m sharing my experience for our juniors that what have I gone through. For BCA/BSC students who are in their last year, Wipro launches their placement drive most probably at the year end.
Wipro hires students for WIPRO- WILP program in which they provide you master’s along with your work from a premier institute of India. As the master’s (MTech) duration last long for four years, Wipro adds a service agreement (bond) with this program i.e. for 5 years. But the plus point here is you will not have to pay anything for this. Until your master’s is completed, the company provides you stipend which increases every year and after completion of master’s you will be converted into an Employee with a great hike in your salary. Now coming to the selection process, you have to go through 3 phases that are:  1) Assessment (verbal, aptitude, reasoning and essay writing)  2) TR round  3) HR round
I followed a website: “indiabix.com”, some YouTube channels such as Campus Monk, Fresher’s world, Career ride etc.  In aptitude, the topics that I would suggest are: (time and work, number system, speed time and distance, ratio and proportion, partnership, SI and PI, probability, profit and loss, mixture and allegations etc.). For reasoning: (blood relation, calendar, clock, direction sensing, arrangements, syllogism etc.). Then comes the essay part in which you have to write an essay based on the given topic. Use backspaces as less a possible.
After clearing first round, you will receive a mail for Technical Round (TR).In both TR and HR communication skills are checked. The in interviewer will judge you based on your skills in IT field and whatever you learned in your college life. For TR, just prepare the basics of all programming languages that you have mentioned in your resume. If you have made any project then it will be a great advantage for you. For me, I was TR was short. He asked about the current trends in IT industries, some questions based on AI, Cloud Computing, C programming etc.
After clearing TR, in HR Round they just check your communication skill, listening skill and they ask questions like, what you know about the company? Why should I hire you? Your strength and weaknesses etc. At last they will confirm all the terms and policies about the WILP program.
That’s it. Now it’s all in their hand, you will get your LOI (Letter Of Intent) if you performed well in all the three rounds. After getting final semester results you have to complete a document verification process. Cheers! You will be joining as a Scholar Trainee. P.S.-(The information shared above may vary in the case, as for us everything was conducted online virtually.)
